Google Ads.
Google Ads is an online advertising platform developed by Google, where advertisers bid to display brief advertisements, 
service offerings, product listings, or videos to web users. It can place ads both in the results of search engines 
like Google Search and on non-search websites, mobile apps, and videos. 
Google Ads is the leading worldwide marketing software because it can reach the highest number of users. 
With Google Ads, you can even specify your target audience like age, location, and service type...
Google Ads is a kind of paid advertisement where you pay for each click with a limit that you set. It's the best bank for your bucks.
It can be used to generate leads or boost your sales.
